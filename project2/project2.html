A couple of questions I am interested in:

  1. Which genre were most of the movies were released in?
  2. Is there any relation between the revenue and some other attributes of the movie mainly budget, runtime, and release year ?
  3. If I want to direct a successful movie with maximum revenue, in how many movies should I require my casts to be in before I recruit them if my movie is in genre X?

Question 3: If I want to direct a successful movie with maximum revenue, in how many movies should I require my casts to be in before I recruit them if my movie is in genre X?¶

So for the final analysis, I'll try to ask a weird question: If I want to direct a successful movie with maximum revenue, in how many movies should I require my casts to be in before I recruit them if my movie is in genre X?

To answer this question: I'll get the following info from the dataset:

  1. Revenue per actor: the actor was part of a successfull movie, either as main or secondary cast
  2. Genre per actor: actors have been in different genres, but they shine the most in one field.
  3. Number of movies each actor have been in

Let's prepare the dataset, it's true that I did copy df_clean to df_test and then performed operation on test. But I could change that in a different version.
